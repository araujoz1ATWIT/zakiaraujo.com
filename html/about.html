What is my passion?

Today, I have truly immersed myself in computer science. I use my curiosity to fuel my drive into computer science. I love the field of computer science because there are almost unlimited things to question and learn. But another thing that I found powerful in computer science was the ability to use computer science to help others in need. I began to use my skills in computer science to make websites and companies to help those in need. One of my first projects was creating a website for Hinche Marathon Organization, my website sought to bring light to the work that they do for children in poverty in Haiti and help carry their efforts further through a modern website. I also used my knowledge of computer science to create a clothing line No Bounds Clothing to give back to communities in need, but also spread positivity. Over time I have been able to develop my curiosity to the point where I am able to focus on a field and immerse myself in it and truly learn everything that crosses my mind.
